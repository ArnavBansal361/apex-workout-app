Privacy Policy

Lift by Tended Studio — Last updated June 26, 2026

Lift is a fitness tracking app built by Tended Studio. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address, used to create and identify your account.
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, weight, and timestamps that you log in the app.
  • Body measurements — bodyweight entries you record over time.
  • Optional integrations — if you connect Spotify or Google Calendar, we access only the data needed to power those features (currently read-only). You can disconnect at any time from the app settings.

How we use your data

  • To provide and personalize the app experience.
  • To display your progress, history, and insights within the app.
  • To power AI features (such as workout parsing and coaching) using your logged data as context.

How your data is stored

All data is stored securely in Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is associated with your account and protected by row-level security — only you can access your own records.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Data is only shared with the infrastructure providers necessary to run the app (Supabase for storage, Anthropic for AI features).

Data retention and deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at hello@tendedstudio.com.

Children

Lift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions or requests: hello@tendedstudio.com
